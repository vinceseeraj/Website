About Anonymous

Anonymous is a hacktivist group created on 4chan.com. There is no known creator and members range from all over the world. Their purpose is to protect the people from their government. Their iconic mask is that of Guy Fawkes. Guy Fawkes is an iconic hero who tried to blow up the England House of Lords in 1605. This was known as the gunpowder plot. Guy Fawkes was captured and later hung.1.
