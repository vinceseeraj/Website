The issue with using MatLab recording is that when trying to speak into the mic, you have to repeat the word over multiple times, in hopes to catch the recording. Since it does not start recording immediately after the display function runs. Furthermore, when it does catch the recording, the sampling rate must be the same as the sampling rate of recording, and even then there may be an issue in identifying depending on how a person said the word. Whether they say the word at a lower volume or higher than normal. Which means more training for the system, or even storing that sample into the codebook to help with identifying who the voice is. However, it is plausible for the system to identify who is speaking as long as it has the voice in the database.
